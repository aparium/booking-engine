Hotel Policies and Procedures

CANCELLATION POLICY
Cancellations must take place by 3 p.m. CST - 24 hours prior to the arrival date or else a cancellation fee of one night’s room and tax will be charged to the credit card on file.

 

CHECK IN

Guest Check-In time is 3 p.m. and Departure time is 11 a.m.. Guests are required to be 21 years of age or older in order to check in to the hotel.

 

SMOKING/VAPING POLICY

Hotel Heron is a non-smoking hotel - a $250.00 fee will be applied for smoking in your guest room. When checking in with your debit card, funds for room, tax and $100.00 per day for incidentals will be held from your checking account until the time of check out.

 

PET POLICY
Dogs are welcome. A $75.00 fee is required at check in to allow us to thoroughly clean and sanitize the room after your departure. No pets over 50 pounds are permitted and no more than two pets are allowed per guest room.

 

DESTINATION AMENTITY FEE

Your daily guest amenities fee of $25 USD plus tax includes a $25 food and beverage credit in KILN, access to Hotel Heron's lifestyle events, fresh Swings coffee each morning in the lobby, unlimited access to fitness center and high speed Wi-Fi Access.
